What is Car Sharing?

Car sharing is a model of car rental where people rent cars for short periods, often by the hour. It provides an alternative to car ownership for individuals and businesses, reducing the need for private car ownership and the associated costs such as maintenance, insurance, and parking.

Car sharing services typically provide access to a fleet of vehicles located throughout a city or region. Users can reserve a vehicle online or through a mobile app, pick up the car from a designated location, and return it after use.
